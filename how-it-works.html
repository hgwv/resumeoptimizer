How it works

Three steps to a recruiter-ready resume.

We combine AI-powered analysis with human expert editing so your resume is tailored to the exact roles and programs you want. Every engagement is built around your goals.

1. Upload + questionnaire

Send us your current resume and answer a short form about your target role or program, grades, and key strengths. We use this to build your optimization plan.

2. AI + expert optimization

Our AI rewrites your bullets for measurable impact, while a resume strategist ensures clarity, cohesion, and ATS compatibility.

3. Delivery + walkthrough

Receive a Google Doc with tracked edits plus a polished PDF/DOCX file. We also include a change summary so you can reuse the framework.

What we need from you

  • Your latest resume (PDF or DOCX)
  • Target role or program
  • Grades, metrics, achievements
  • Strengths you want to highlight

What you get back

  • Google Doc with tracked edits + comments
  • Final polished resume file
  • ATS-ready keyword list
  • Executive summary you can reuse
