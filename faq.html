FAQ

Your questions, answered.

Everything you need to know about ResumeGlow AI before you submit your resume.

How long does it take?

Standard delivery is 48 hours. Executive plans include a 24-hour turnaround.

Will you change my formatting?

Yes. We optimize for ATS compatibility while keeping your resume clean and easy to read for recruiters.

Can I request revisions?

Each plan includes at least one revision pass. Executive plans include two rounds of updates.

Do you handle academic CVs?

We specialize in one-page or two-page resumes for industry roles, internships, and graduate programs.

What happens after checkout?

You receive an email confirming your order, and we start optimizing your resume. You'll get a Google Doc with tracked edits and the final file once complete.
